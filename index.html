In this unit we were allowed to choose an area that interested us. I have always been fasinated by Virtual Reality and it is a bit different than my usual preferance of coding. We first completed a research task, in which I learnt alot about what Virtual Reality is and it's postives and negitives. Then we submitted a project plan. I started my unit simple; 3D pictures. I learnt to use the schools GoPro Fusion 360 which allowed me to take pictures around the school. Then you had to blend the pictures, to make a 360 veiw and I uploaded them to a site called Google Tours. Google Tours allows you to get these images from google maps or upload your own. Putting the images together I could create a virtual tour of the school. Once I had finished this we still had 2 weeks left, so I got to move on and explore other elements of Virtual Reality. In my opinion this was the fun bit! I used the schools headset with a feature called Google Blocks. This allows you to build scenes and objects in 3D while on the headset. The tecnology is amazing. I spent a lesson watching youtube videos to learn the very basics and then had a go. I (admitiedly) spent a good 5 minutes building 'brick towers' like a 2 year old being absolutely fasinated and getting the hang of it. Then I tried other features like customising the size, shape and colour of the objects. I, then, decided to try and make something more complex- a human. I have to admit, I am not the most artistic person and I was still getting the hang of it, so it didn't look very good. Moving on I tried to make a simple block like horse; which despite not being scientifically acurate or proportionate, looked ok. I found this unit alot of fun and learnt a whole new side to art/ games/ worlds and digital technology I was oblivious to. I faced small issues with downloading or learning to use the camera, but nothing dramatic- just what you would expect when you have no idea how to do something. I think I worked well and I could improve next time mainly with my increased knowlage, meaning I could move on to more complex things. Looking back on this unit the only thing I would have done differently would be to spend more time useing GoogleBlocks because that was really fun.
